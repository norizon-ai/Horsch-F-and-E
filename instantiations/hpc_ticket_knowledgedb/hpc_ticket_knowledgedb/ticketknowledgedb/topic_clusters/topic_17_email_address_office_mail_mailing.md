# HPC Support Ticket: Account Extension for ungr01

## Keywords
- Account extension
- Email address change
- IDM (Identity Management)
- LDAP (Lightweight Directory Access Protocol)
- Mail forwarding

## Summary
A request was made to extend the account `ungr01` for one year and to update the email address associated with the account.

## Root Cause
- The user requested an account extension and an update to the email address.
- The current email address was not the preferred one, and the user wanted to use an external email address from the PDF application.

## Solution
- The account `ungr01` was extended until December 31, 2011.
- The email address could not be directly changed to an external one due to LDAP restrictions.
- The user was advised to forward emails from the official university address to the preferred external address via the IDM web interface.
- An activation letter was sent to the user to enable them to change the email forwarding settings.

## What Can Be Learned
- Official university email addresses are typically used in LDAP.
- Users can forward emails from their official university address to an external address via the IDM web interface.
- Account extensions can be processed even if the user is inactive in IDM, but new entries require an active IDM account.
- Communication and coordination between different support teams (e.g., HPC Admins and 2nd Level Support) are crucial for resolving complex issues.
